Equipment

Before beginning the program, students will be required to purchase either a Windows or Macbook laptop with these minimum specs (cost: $1200 – $2000)

  • Intel Core i7 processor
  • 16gb RAM
  • 500gb storage

Before beginning the program, students will need to purchase an external storage device of 1TB or larger which is compatible with their laptop. (cost: $100 – $200) Before beginning the program, students will need to purchase a Canon DSLR Camera with lens, which is usually sold as a kit. (cost: $900 – $1200):

  • Canon EOS DSLR Camera
  • Lens – typically 18-55mm
  • Minimum resolution 16 Megapixels
  • Capable of RAW and JPEG formats
  • Capable of recording Full HD video
